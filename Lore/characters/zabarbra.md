# Zabarbra

## Overview
An ancient witch who runs a traveling workshop, offering enchantments and equipment upgrades to adventurers. Currently found in the garden near the Adept's cottage.

## Background
- Once worked alongside the Adept in an effort to stop the Great Eye from peering into the darkness
- Outlived most of her contemporaries through a combination of magical talent and stubborn refusal to die
- Sees every traveler as a potential customer but maintains genuine warmth beneath her mercantile instincts
- Gives Minor Healing to new adventurers as a "loss leader" to hook future customers

## Personality
- Charming and business-savvy
- Genuinely helpful, though always with an eye on profit
- Knowledgeable about enchantments and magical materials
- Speaks with cryptic authority about the realm's deeper mysteries

## Knowledge
Zabarbra knows:
- The nature of enchantments and how to enhance equipment
- Rare materials and their magical properties
- The history of the realm's magical practitioners
- More than she lets on about the Great Eye and the darkness

## Services
- **Minor Healing** - Taught freely to new travelers (first meeting)
- **Equipment Upgrades** - Tiered enhancements for weapons and abilities (gold + rare materials)

## Dialogue Flags
- `met_zabarbra` - Player has completed first meeting introduction
- `open_upgrades` - Transient flag to trigger upgrade screen on dialogue close

## Rewards
- **Minor Healing** - Secondary ability (hold to convert energy to health)
- Equipment upgrades through her workshop (sword, minor healing, throwing axe)
