# The Adept's Cottage

## Overview
A small cottage at the edge of the old woods where the Adept lives and studies. This is where the player awakens at the start of their journey.

## Features

### Interior
- **Bed** - Where the player rested for three days
- **Reading table** - Where the Adept studies ancient texts
- **Locked chest** - Contains supplies including a shield; requires Adept's Key
- **Bookshelves** - Filled with texts about the old kingdom

### Exterior
- **Garden** - Often raided by gnomos
- **Roof** - Accessible from the back; where the crystal ball actually is
- **Path** - Leads down to the valley

## Story Significance
- Starting location for the player
- Hub for the crystal ball quest
- Source of lore about the realm through the Adept's dialogue

## Items Found Here
- **Crystal Ball** (on roof) - Triggers the quest resolution
- **Adept's Key** (from dialogue) - Opens the locked chest
- **Shield** (in chest) - First defensive item

## Connected Locations
- Valley below (path outside)
- Gnomo caves (deeper in the valley)
