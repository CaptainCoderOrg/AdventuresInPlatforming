KNIGHTMARE

A 2D action-platformer Made in 37 days for the Solo Development Marathon Jam #5.

You wake in a small cottage at the edge of the woods. You don't remember who you are, where you came from, or how you got here. An old scholar tells you he found you collapsed near the cliffside. You have no belongings, no identity... only a strange energy coursing through you.

Controls

Supports Gamepad Controller or Keyboard & Mouse. Default controls listed below but can be set in the settings menu.

  • Move: WASD / D-PAD
  • Jump: Space / Gamepad South
  • Attack: Mouse Left / Gamepad West
  • Interact: Up Arrow / D-pad Up (NPCs, campfires, chests)

Tips for Success

  • Rest at campfires to save, heal, and upgrade your stats!
  • If you're lost, check the map
  • If you're not sure what to do next, consult your journal
  • Enemies too tough? Don't forget to upgrade your equipment with Zabarbra
  • Enemies still too tough? You can change the difficulty in the settings menu mid game
